## ğŸ“‹ Parameter Quick Guide

### extract_code_examples
- `query` - What to find
- `language` - "csharp" | "javascript" | "any"
- `max_examples` - 1-10 (default: 5)

### get_method_signatures
- `class_name` - e.g., "Transform"
- `method_name` - Search across all classes
- `static_only` - true/false

### search_by_use_case
- `use_case` - "make player jump"
- `experience_level` - "beginner" | "intermediate" | "advanced"
- `prefer_code` - true/false

### get_full_documents
- `query` - Search term
- `max_documents` - 1-10 (default: 3)
- `doc_type` - "manual" | "script_reference" | "both"

### get_related_documents
- `class_name` - e.g., "MonoBehaviour"
- `topic` - Alternative to class_name
- `include_inheritance` - true/false
- `max_related` - 1-5
